Our Mission

Thurmont Education is intent on supporting the physical, mental, spiritual and leadership development of individuals and communities. Through education, health fairs, and individual coaching, we empower people to live their best lives by assisting in achieving optimal wellness

Our Vision

  • Provide learning communities with tools to assist in their overall wellbeing
  • Hold community lectures on topics ranging from health promotion, interfaith dialogue and public health issues
  • Offer grief counseling utilizing a variety of therapeutic methods
  • Provide community members with leadership development tools
  • Offer health fairs for all community members including the underserved and immigrant populations by partnering with social service groups, medical professionals and schools of nursing
